## Share tokens
In DeFi shares are very often represented as tokens (mostly commonly ERC20).

### Acquiring share tokens
- You get share tokens when you deposit assets to a vault (primary market).
- You could also buy share tokens on secondary markets.

### Redeeming share tokens
- You can redeem the vault assets that your share tokens worth from the vault (primary market).
- You could also sell your share tokens on secondary markets.

### Properties
- Your share token track a `proportional` right to withdraw assets from the vault.
- When you add or remove funds, shares mint or burn to keep proportions fair, `ensuring no one dilutes others unfairly`.

eg if you hold 1% of total shares, you claim 1% of the pool's value or earnings.

### Number
The share token number that represents the proportion does not matter, as long as the proportion is correct.
- 10 share tokens in 100 total circulating = 10%
- 100 share tokens in 1000 total circulating = 10%

### Value
This is how you determine the value of your share:

$ \normalsize{\text{1 Share token value in vault assets}} =
\frac { \normalsize{\text{total vault assets}} } { \normalsize{\text{total circulating supply of share tokens}} } $


<Aside type="caution">Integer division looses fractional part. Increase precision or take other measurements.</Aside>

<Aside type="caution">Take care for division by zero.</Aside>

## Shares (LP tokens)

Shares are the representation of liquidity in a liquidity pool.

They are created when you add liquidity to a pool and are used to track your share of the pool's liquidity.

Shares are also used to calculate the amount of tokens you receive when you remove liquidity from a pool.

The core thing to remember here is that:
- The value of one LP token should always be equal to the total value of the pool divided by the number of LP tokens in circulation.
- Adding or removing liquidity should not in any way change the value of other people’s LP tokens.

To find the value of the LP tokens, you divide the total pool value by the total number of LP tokens.

The number does not matter as long as the value is fair.
